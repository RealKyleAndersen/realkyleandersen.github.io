2.1 Equity in AI

Generative AI is a smart tool that can make things that look real but aren't. It's like a fancy artist that can create pictures, words, and videos that seem true. But sometimes, this AI is used to make stuff up, like fake news. Deep fakes are a part of this AI that make videos that look real but aren't. They can show people saying or doing things they never really did. This can be a big problem because it tricks people and can be used to spread lies. The bad effects of this can be huge. It can make people doubt what they see and hear, especially from important people or the news. It can also make people fight or not trust each other. To stop the fake stuff, we need to tell people about it, so they know what to watch out for. We should also tell the tech companies to use their smart AI to find and say, "Hey, this is fake!" Laws or rules can be made to punish those who use this AI to fool others. Teamwork between regular people, smart tech people, and the people making the rules is super important to fix this problem.
